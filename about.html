About Me

Hello! Thanks for stopping by. I'm a UX/UI designer based in Madrid that loves designing physical and digital experiences. I have always been fascinated by technology, but my interest in design emerged close to finishing my bachelor's degree in computer engineering.

I had my first work experience during my master's in Human-Computer Interaction and Design, where I tackled real-world problems and users. More recently, I also had the opportunity to redesign the experience and interface of a music streaming platform from Nigeria.

I consider myself a very organized and cheerful person with an interest in new design trends and technologies. I'm always trying to grow and learn something new. Please, don't hesitate to reach out!
