Poly Treasure Island
Explore 3D Model

Poly Treasure Island is the outcome of a short online course about 3D modeling in Blender. The idea of this project was to get familiarized with the Blender interface, as well as understanding the modeling theory.

The project was divided into three phases: First, the different elements from the scene were modeled starting from either cubes or cylinders. To achieve the desired results, different tools such as extruding or applying modifiers were used.

Once the models were complete and correctly placed, the different materials were applied to each object. All elements within the scene except for the treasure chest have a non-metallic and non-reflective texture.

Lastly, the camera was set up, as well as all the different rendering settings. Once the render was completed, I adjusted the color levels and added a background using Affinity Photo.
